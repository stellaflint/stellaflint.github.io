Sports stores

All these stores can be found in auckland. Nike stores are usually found in the city or in a mall and rebel sports are usually placed beside Briscoes.


Nike Inc. (NKE) is a global footwear and apparel company that designs, develops, markets, and sells athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. Although primarily designed for athletic use, many of its products are worn for casual or leisure activities.
Rebel Sport is a sport gear and equipment company that supplies brands from all over the world. They sell products from basketballs to weight training and mouthguards. These stalls can be found all over Newzealand with 10 stores in Auckland.
Stirling sports is a streetwear company, where they offer everything to do with basketball and running equipment. They sell merchandise from NBA and NCAA as well as a wide range of running gear from Nike and Adidas.
Torpedo7 is a mjor brand in NZ, where they stock the largest rangoe of supplies for outdoor activities. They sell snow suplies, kayaking, surf and skating. they also provide top end brands like northface and Edmund Hillary.
